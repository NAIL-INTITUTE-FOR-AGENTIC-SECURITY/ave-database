# NAIL Institute — Academic Programme

Building the next generation of agentic AI security researchers and practitioners
through university partnerships, curriculum resources, and student engagement.

## Mission

Bridge the gap between academic AI safety research and real-world agentic AI
security by providing structured educational pathways, research collaboration
frameworks, and a pipeline of trained talent for the community.

## Programme Pillars

### 1. [University Course Modules](courses/)
Ready-to-adopt course modules that universities can integrate into existing
CS, AI, and cybersecurity curricula.

### 2. [PhD Research Partnership](research/)
Structured collaboration between NAIL and doctoral researchers working on
agentic AI security topics.

### 3. [Student Ambassador Programme](ambassadors/)
Empowering students to lead agentic AI security awareness and research on
their campuses.

### 4. [Academic Advisory Board](advisory-board.md)
Distinguished academics providing strategic guidance on research direction
and educational standards.
