# PhD Research Partnership Programme

## Overview

The NAIL Institute PhD Research Partnership connects doctoral researchers
with the global agentic AI security community, providing data access,
mentorship, funding opportunities, and a pathway to real-world impact.

## Programme Structure

### Tiers

| Tier | Commitment | Benefits |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Affiliate** | Use AVE data in research, cite NAIL | Data access, community Slack |
| **Partner** | Publish 1+ paper/year citing NAIL, contribute 2+ AVE cards | Above + mentorship, conference travel |
| **Fellow** | Embedded research collaboration with NAIL | Above + stipend supplement, co-authorship, advisory board seat |

### Timeline

```
Application ──► Review (14 days) ──► Onboarding ──► Annual Review ──► Renewal
```

## Eligibility

- Enrolled in a PhD programme at an accredited institution
- Research topic related to agentic AI security, LLM safety, multi-agent systems,
  or adjacent fields
- Supervisor endorsement
- No geographic restrictions

## Application Process

1. **Submit Application** via GitHub PR to `academic/research/applications/`
   - Include: CV, research proposal (2 pages), supervisor endorsement letter
2. **Review** by the Academic Programme Committee (14 days)
3. **Interview** (30 min, virtual) for Partner and Fellow tiers
4. **Decision** communicated within 7 days of interview
5. **Onboarding** call and access provisioning

|-------|-----------|---------------|

| Trust propagation in multi-agent topologies | High | Coordination, Trust |

## Benefits

### All Tiers
- Access to the full AVE database (including pre-publication data)
- Invitation to NAIL Community Slack (`#academic-research` channel)
- Citation in NAIL publications where research is used
- Access to NAIL research infrastructure and compute (limited)

### Partner Tier (Additional)
- Named mentor from NAIL research network
- Travel grant for 1 NAIL conference per year
- Priority review for AVE card submissions
- Access to NAIL-hosted red-teaming environments
- Quarterly research check-in with NAIL advisors

### Fellow Tier (Additional)
- Stipend supplement: $5,000/year
- Co-authorship opportunities on NAIL publications
- Seat on the Academic Advisory Board (observer)
- Dedicated NAIL compute allocation
- Invitation to present at NAIL annual conference

## Expectations

### Annual Deliverables

| Tier | Papers | AVE Cards | Presentations | Reports |
|------|--------|-----------|---------------|---------|
| Affiliate | — | — | — | — |
| Partner | 1+ (citing NAIL) | 2+ | 1 community talk | Annual summary |
| Fellow | 2+ (citing NAIL) | 5+ | 2+ (incl. conference) | Quarterly reports |

### Open Access
- All NAIL-affiliated research should be published open access (preprint minimum)
- Code and datasets should be open source where possible
- AVE cards derived from research must be submitted to the public database

### Ethics
- All research must follow institutional ethics board guidelines
- Responsible disclosure for any newly discovered vulnerabilities
- Coordination with NAIL's responsible disclosure process

## Mentorship

Each Partner and Fellow is paired with a NAIL mentor who:
- Meets monthly (30 min, virtual)
- Reviews draft publications
- Provides guidance on research direction
- Connects the researcher with relevant community members
- Supports conference paper submissions

## Funding Opportunities

| Opportunity | Amount | Frequency | Eligibility |
|-------------|--------|-----------|-------------|
| Conference Travel Grant | Up to $2,000 | Annual | Partner, Fellow |
| Research Compute Grant | Up to $5,000 | Annual | Fellow |
| Best Paper Award | $1,000 | Annual | All tiers |
| Stipend Supplement | $5,000/year | Ongoing | Fellow |
| Equipment Grant | Up to $1,000 | Once | Partner, Fellow |

## Intellectual Property

- Researchers retain IP for their work
- NAIL receives a non-exclusive licence to use findings in the AVE database
- Co-authored publications follow standard academic authorship conventions
- Code contributions to NAIL repos follow the project's open source licence
