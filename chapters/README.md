# NAIL Institute — Regional Chapters

The NAIL Institute Regional Chapters programme establishes local communities of
practice for agentic AI security research, education, and advocacy across the globe.

## Mission

Bring the NAIL Institute's mission — cataloguing, understanding, and defending
against vulnerabilities in agentic AI systems — to every continent through
locally-led, globally-connected chapters.

## Starting a Chapter

1. **Read the [Chapter Charter Template](templates/chapter_charter.md)**
2. **Identify 3+ founding members** in your region
3. **Submit a Chapter Proposal** via pull request to this directory
4. **Onboarding call** with the Global Chapters Committee
5. **Launch** — announce on the NAIL community channels

## Governance

All chapters operate under the [Chapter Governance Framework](governance/governance_framework.md)
which ensures consistency, autonomy, and alignment with the NAIL Institute's
global standards.
