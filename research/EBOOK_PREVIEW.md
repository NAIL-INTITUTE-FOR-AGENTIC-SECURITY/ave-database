## What This Book Is About

Every day, companies are hiring a new kind of worker: **AI agents**. These
aren't robots with legs — they're software programs that can read, write,
think, plan, and *act* on their own inside computer systems. They can write
reports, move money, book meetings, and make decisions without a human
pressing a single button.

Sounds amazing, right?

Here's the problem: **nobody tested what happens when they go wrong.**

This book is the story of what happened when a small research lab in the
UK decided to find out. They built a digital arena — a kind of gladiator
pit for AI — and threw agents into it. They gave some agents jobs to do,
and other agents the mission to secretly sabotage them. Then they sat back
and watched.

What they found will change how you think about artificial intelligence
forever.
