# 🎨 Brand Guidelines — Conference Use

## Brand Identity

### Name
- **Full**: NAIL Institute for Agentic Security
- **Short**: NAIL Institute
- **Abbreviation**: NAIL (acceptable in headers/badges)
- **Never**: "Nail", "nail", "N.A.I.L."

### Tagline
> *Securing the Autonomous Future*

### Positioning Statement
The NAIL Institute is the leading open-source community for
cataloguing, scoring, and defending against vulnerabilities in
autonomous AI systems.

## Logo Usage

### Minimum Sizes
- **Full logo**: 120px wide (digital), 30mm wide (print)
- **Icon only**: 32px wide (digital), 10mm wide (print)

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "N" in NAIL on all
sides of the logo.

### Do Not
- ❌ Stretch or distort the logo
- ❌ Rotate the logo
- ❌ Change logo colours outside approved palette
- ❌ Place logo on busy backgrounds without overlay
- ❌ Add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- ❌ Use the logo smaller than minimum size

## Conference-Specific Assets

### Booth Backdrop (2400×800mm)
- Dark navy background (`#1a1a2e`)
- Logo centred, large format
- Tagline below logo
- QR code to nailinstitute.org (bottom-right)
- No more than 5 key stats

### Pull-Up Banner (800×2000mm)
- Same navy background
- Vertical layout: Logo → Tagline → 3 bullet points → QR code
- Bullet points: "50+ Vulnerabilities", "Open Source", "Community-Driven"

### Table Banner (1800×800mm)
- Logo left, URL right
- Single-line tagline centre

### Badge/Lanyard
- NAIL Electric background
- White NAIL logo
- Speaker/Sponsor/Attendee designation

## Slide Deck Standards

### Title Slide
- Navy background
- Logo top-left (small)
- Talk title centre (Inter Bold, 44pt)
- Speaker name + date bottom
- Conference logo bottom-right (if permitted)

### Content Slides
- White background with navy header bar
- Section headers: Inter Bold, 28pt, NAIL Navy
- Body: Source Sans Pro, 20pt
- Maximum 6 bullet points per slide
- Code blocks: JetBrains Mono on `#2f3542` background

### Demo Slides
- Terminal screenshot or live demo
- Green "LIVE DEMO" badge top-right
- Minimal surrounding text

### Closing Slide
- Navy background
- "Thank You" centre
- Contact information
- QR code to relevant resource
- Social handles

## Photography & Video

### Style
- Clean, professional, tech-forward
- Blue/dark colour grading preferred
- Show diversity in people and settings
- Avoid stock photo aesthetics

### Attribution
- All NAIL-created media: CC BY 4.0
- Third-party media: credit required
- Conference photos: respect event policies
