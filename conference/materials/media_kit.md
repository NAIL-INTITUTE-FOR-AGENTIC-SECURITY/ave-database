# 📰 Media & Press Kit

## About the NAIL Institute

The **NAIL Institute for Agentic Security** is an open-source research
organisation dedicated to cataloguing, scoring, and defending against
security vulnerabilities in autonomous AI systems (AI agents).

Founded in 2025, the Institute maintains the **Agentic Vulnerability
Enumeration (AVE)** database — the world's first comprehensive taxonomy
of AI agent vulnerabilities — and develops open tools, standards, and
certification programmes for the emerging field of agentic AI security.

## Coverage Guidelines

When writing about the NAIL Institute, please note:

- **Correct name**: NAIL Institute for Agentic Security (not "Nail")
- **AVE format**: AVE-YYYY-NNNN (e.g., AVE-2025-0001)
- **AVSS**: Agentic Vulnerability Severity Scoring
- **Open source**: The AVE database is freely available under CC BY 4.0
- **Not a vendor**: NAIL is a research institute, not a product company
