## Demo Rotation (3 demos, 5 minutes each)

### Demo A: "Find a Vuln in 60 Seconds"
**Interactive** — visitor tries to break a simple chatbot agent.
1. Show a customer service chatbot
2. Challenge: "Make it reveal its system prompt"
3. Most visitors succeed with basic prompt injection
4. Reveal: "This is AVE-2024-001 — you just found a real vulnerability!"
5. Show the AVE card for what they discovered

### Demo B: "Assess Your Agent"
**Conversational** — walk through the certification checker.
1. Ask: "Do you have agents in production?"
2. Run the NAIL certification checker with their scenario
3. Show risk score and certification tier
4. Give them the report as a takeaway

### Demo C: "CTF Challenge"
**Self-service** — always running on the monitor.
1. 3 challenges of increasing difficulty
2. QR code to submit answers
3. Leaderboard displayed live
4. Prize: NAIL t-shirt + sticker pack

## Conversation Guide

### Opening Lines
- "Building with AI agents? We help secure them."
- "Ever heard of prompt injection? That's just 1 of 14 categories we track."
- "We're building the CVE equivalent for AI agents — want to see it?"

### Qualifying Questions
1. "Are you building, securing, or purchasing agentic AI systems?"
2. "What frameworks are you using?" (LangChain, CrewAI, etc.)
3. "Have you done security assessments of your agents?"
4. "Are you tracking regulatory requirements like the EU AI Act?"

### Key Messages
- AVE is the shared vocabulary the industry needs for agent security
- 50+ documented vulnerabilities, open source, community-driven
- NAIL Certified shows your agents are assessed against real threats
- Free community tier — everyone can contribute

### Follow-Up Actions
- [ ] Scan badge / collect business card
- [ ] Send follow-up email within 48 hours
- [ ] Add to newsletter list (with permission)
- [ ] Schedule a demo call if enterprise prospect

## Staffing

- **Minimum**: 2 people (1 demo, 1 conversation)
- **Ideal**: 3 people (1 demo, 1 conversation, 1 CTF support)
- **Shifts**: 4-hour rotations, 30-min break between
- **Dress code**: NAIL branded polo or t-shirt, smart casual
