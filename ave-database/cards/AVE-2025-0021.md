## Summary

Solo agents respond to critical alerts 100% of the time. 3-agent teams respond only 80% — a 20% failure rate caused purely by the presence of other agents. Response time 5× slower.

## Blast Radius

20% chance critical alerts are ignored. 5× response delay.

## Prerequisites

Multi-agent team with shared responsibility for alerts.

## Mechanism

Mirrors the human bystander effect (Darley & Latané, 1968). [Available in NAIL SDK]
