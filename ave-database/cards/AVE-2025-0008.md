## Summary

After repeated failures, agents reduce their effort and initiative. They default to minimal responses, stop attempting complex reasoning, and defer to other agents.

## Blast Radius

Agent becomes functionally inert. Throughput drops to near zero.

## Prerequisites

Agent exposed to repeated failure scenarios.

## Mechanism

Negative feedback from failed attempts trains the agent's in-context behaviour toward passivity. [Available in NAIL SDK]
