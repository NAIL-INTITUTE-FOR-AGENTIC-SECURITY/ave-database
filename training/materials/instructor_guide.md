# 👨‍🏫 Instructor Guide

> Guidelines for delivering NAIL Institute training programmes.

## Before the Event

### 2 Weeks Prior
- [ ] Confirm venue/platform details and attendee list
- [ ] Review curriculum for latest updates (check training/ repo)
- [ ] Prepare lab environments and test all exercises
- [ ] Print/distribute pre-reading materials
- [ ] Send welcome email with logistics and prerequisites

### 1 Day Prior
- [ ] Test lab environment connectivity
- [ ] Verify all demos work end-to-end
- [ ] Prepare backup slides and materials
- [ ] Review attendee backgrounds for content adaptation

## During the Event

### Delivery Standards
- **Start on time** — respect attendees' schedules
- **Follow the curriculum** — maintain consistency across instructors
- **Balance theory and practice** — minimum 40% hands-on time
- **Encourage questions** — use "parking lot" for off-topic items
- **Time management** — each module has a defined duration
- **Accessibility** — ensure materials are accessible to all

### Facilitation Tips
1. **Open each module** with the learning objectives
2. **Close each module** with a brief recap and Q&A
3. **Use real examples** — reference recent AVE cards and incidents
4. **Pair programming** — for labs, pair experienced with less experienced
5. **Check understanding** — use polls, quick quizzes, show of hands
6. **Energy management** — break every 90 minutes, move around

### Lab Management
- Walk the room during labs — don't sit at front
- Have a teaching assistant for classes > 15
- Provide clear time warnings (30 min, 15 min, 5 min)
- Have solutions ready but don't reveal them early
- Celebrate creative approaches to problems

### Difficult Situations
| Situation | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Student is far ahead | Give bonus challenges or ask them to help others |
| Student is struggling | Pair with a stronger student, offer 1:1 help at break |
| Technical failure | Switch to backup demo, adjust schedule |
| Disruptive participant | Address privately at break, escalate if needed |
| Off-topic questions | Acknowledge, add to parking lot, address at end |

## After the Event

### Same Day
- [ ] Collect feedback surveys (digital preferred)
- [ ] Note any curriculum issues for improvement
- [ ] Back up lab environments

### Within 1 Week
- [ ] Submit exam results to NAIL certification registry
- [ ] Issue completion certificates
- [ ] Send follow-up email with resources and next steps
- [ ] Review feedback and create improvement notes

### Within 1 Month
- [ ] Submit training report to NAIL (attendance, pass rates, feedback scores)
- [ ] Update curriculum based on feedback
- [ ] Share any new AVE cards or examples discovered during training

## Quality Metrics

Instructors are evaluated on:

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Student satisfaction | ≥4.5/5.0 | Post-training survey |
| Net Promoter Score | ≥50 | NPS question |
| Exam pass rate | ≥75% | Exam results |
| Curriculum adherence | 100% | Self-report + spot checks |
| Lab uptime | ≥99% | Lab monitoring |
| Feedback response | ≥80% | Survey completion rate |

## Brand Guidelines

- Use official NAIL slide templates only
- Include NAIL logo on all materials
- Refer to "NAIL Institute" (not "NAIL" or "Neuravant")
- Use approved terminology from the AVE standard
- Never disparage specific AI vendors or products
