# 🎓 NAIL Institute — Training & Workshops Programme

> Instructor-led training for NAIL Certified Assessors and AI security
> professionals.

## Overview

The NAIL Training Programme equips security professionals, AI engineers,
and compliance officers with the knowledge and skills to assess agentic
AI systems against the AVE standard, achieve NAIL Certification, and
build robust defences.

## Training Tracks

### Track 1: AVE Foundations (1 day)
**Audience**: Security analysts, AI engineers, product managers
**Prerequisites**: Basic understanding of AI/ML concepts
**Certification**: NAIL Associate (upon passing exam)

| Module | Duration | Topic |
|--------|----------|-------|
| M1 | 90 min | Introduction to Agentic AI Vulnerabilities |
| M2 | 90 min | The AVE Taxonomy — 14 Categories Deep Dive |
| M3 | 60 min | AVSS Scoring System |
| M4 | 60 min | Using the AVE Database & API |
| M5 | 60 min | **Lab**: Finding Vulnerabilities in Sample Agents |
| Exam | 60 min | NAIL Associate Certification Exam |

### Track 2: NAIL Assessor Certification (3 days)
**Audience**: Security assessors, red teamers, compliance auditors
**Prerequisites**: Track 1 or equivalent experience
**Certification**: NAIL Certified Assessor (NCA)

| Day | Module | Duration | Topic |
|-----|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | M1 | 90 min | AVE Assessment Methodology |
| 1 | M2 | 90 min | Prompt Injection & Goal Drift — Attack & Defence |
| 1 | M3 | 90 min | Tool Misuse & Privilege Escalation — Attack & Defence |
| 1 | M4 | 90 min | **Lab**: Red Teaming a Multi-Agent System |
| 2 | M5 | 90 min | Memory Poisoning & Data Exfiltration |
| 2 | M6 | 90 min | Supply Chain & Coordination Failures |
| 2 | M7 | 90 min | Regulatory Compliance Mapping |
| 2 | M8 | 90 min | **Lab**: Full Assessment of a Production-like Agent |
| 3 | M9 | 90 min | Writing AVE Cards & Responsible Disclosure |
| 3 | M10 | 90 min | Insurance Risk Scoring & Reporting |
| 3 | M11 | 120 min | **Capstone Lab**: End-to-End Assessment |
| 3 | Exam | 120 min | NCA Certification Exam (practical + written) |

### Track 3: Enterprise Workshop (1 day, on-site)
**Audience**: Security teams, engineering leads, CISOs
**Prerequisites**: None
**Certification**: Completion certificate

| Module | Duration | Topic |
|--------|----------|-------|
| M1 | 60 min | Executive Briefing: Agentic AI Risk Landscape |
| M2 | 90 min | Threat Modelling Your AI Systems with AVE |
| M3 | 60 min | Building an AVE Defence Programme |
| M4 | 90 min | **Workshop**: Assess Your Own Systems |
| M5 | 60 min | Certification Roadmap & Quick Wins |
| M6 | 60 min | Q&A and Action Planning |

### Track 4: Train-the-Trainer (2 days)
**Audience**: Training providers, consultants
**Prerequisites**: NAIL Certified Assessor (NCA)
**Certification**: NAIL Authorised Instructor (NAI)

| Day | Module | Duration | Topic |
|-----|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | M1 | 90 min | NAIL Training Standards & Quality Framework |
| 1 | M2 | 90 min | Curriculum Design & Delivery Methods |
| 1 | M3 | 90 min | Lab Environment Setup & Management |
| 1 | M4 | 90 min | Assessment & Exam Administration |
| 2 | M5 | 180 min | **Practice**: Deliver a Module to Peers |
| 2 | M6 | 90 min | Feedback, Evaluation, and Improvement |
| 2 | M7 | 90 min | Business Operations & Partnership Model |
