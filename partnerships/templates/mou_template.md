This Memorandum of Understanding ("MOU") establishes a framework for
collaboration between the Parties in the field of agentic AI security,
specifically regarding the Agentic Vulnerability Enumeration (AVE)
standard and related activities.

This MOU establishes a **[Observer / Collaborator / Strategic / Treaty]**
partnership as defined in the NAIL Institute Partnership Policy v1.0.0.

### 6. DATA SHARING

6.1 Data shared under this MOU shall be governed by the separately
executed Data Sharing Agreement (Annex A).

6.2 All shared data shall comply with applicable data protection
regulations (GDPR, CCPA, or equivalent).

6.3 Vulnerability data shared before public disclosure is subject
to responsible disclosure timelines as defined in NAIL's Security Policy.

### 7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

7.1 Pre-existing IP remains the property of the originating Party.

7.2 Joint works shall be licensed as follows unless otherwise agreed:
- Documentation and research: CC-BY-SA-4.0
- Software and tools: Apache-2.0

7.3 Neither Party shall use the other's trademarks without prior
written consent.

### 8. CONFIDENTIALITY

8.1 Confidential information disclosed under this MOU shall be
protected for a period of 3 years from disclosure.

8.2 Confidential information does not include:
- Information already in the public domain
- Information independently developed
- Information required to be disclosed by law

### 11. AMENDMENT

This MOU may be amended by mutual written agreement of both Parties.
