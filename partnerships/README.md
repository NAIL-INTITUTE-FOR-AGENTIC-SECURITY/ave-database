# 🌍 NAIL Institute — International Partnerships

> Formal collaboration framework for partnering with national AI safety
> bodies, standards organisations, and research institutes worldwide.

## Overview

The NAIL Institute's International Partnerships programme establishes
structured relationships with organisations that share our mission of
making agentic AI systems safer through vulnerability enumeration,
standardised testing, and open research.

## Partnership Tiers

| Tier | Name | Commitment | Benefits |
|------|------|------------|----------|
| 🥉 | **Observer** | Attend quarterly briefings | Early access to AVE advisories, read-only API |
| 🥈 | **Collaborator** | Contribute data / research | Co-branded publications, data-sharing pipeline |
| 🥇 | **Strategic Partner** | Joint programmes, co-funding | Governance seat, priority RFC input, joint events |
| 💎 | **Treaty Partner** | Formal MOU, regulatory alignment | Mutual recognition of certifications, policy drafting |

## Getting Started

1. Review the [Partnership Policy](framework/PARTNERSHIP_POLICY.md)
2. Check tier requirements in [tiers.yaml](framework/tiers.yaml)
3. Submit a [Partnership Proposal](templates/partnership_proposal.md) via GitHub Issue
4. Our Advisory Board reviews proposals quarterly
